Wishlist

This page contains some of the model kits I want to build in the future.

30MS SIS-Hc106K Belveria-Berys

I plan to make another 30 Minutes Sisters custom build, and I want to use Belveria-Berys as the base kit.

HG IBO Carta's Graze Ritter

I like the regal look of Carta's Graze Ritter with the sword and head crest. I might also get some of the grunt versions to have a little army of them since they're pretty cheap.

MG 00 Gundam Kyrios

I don't have any Gundam 00 kits, I'd like to grab this one because of the orange and white colour scheme + the unique claw weapon.

MG IBO Gundam Vidar

I like the Vidar's purgable rapier weapon, and it just looks cool overall.

HG GQ Police Zaku

I planned to get a Zaku at one point or another, and I found the GQuuuuuuX redesign to look the most interesting.

HG IBO Gundam Asmoday

I like Gundam Asmoday's tonfas, and I want more IBO Gundams in my collection.

HG WFM Gundam Lfrith Thorn

I already have the Lfrith Ur, so I'd like to have its sister mobile suit.

HG WFM Beguir-Pente

The Beguir-Pente has a really cool expandable shield, and I want to expand my Witch From Mercury collection.
